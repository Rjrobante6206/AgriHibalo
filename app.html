About AgriHibalo

A community-based platform connecting students and practitioners through knowledge sharing.

01

What is AgriHibalo?

AgriHibalo is a Brainly-inspired Q&A platform built for agriculture and animal science in the Philippines. Students post farming and livestock questions; experienced farmers and practitioners answer. Every helpful contribution earns points, building a culture of collaborative learning.

02

Two Domains

  • 🌾 Farming — soil, crops, irrigation, pest control, fertilizers, harvest
  • 🐄 Animal Science — livestock, poultry, veterinary advice, feeds, breeding
03

How it works

  1. Post your question with a photo
  2. Get answers from verified practitioners
  3. Mark the best answer
  4. Earn points & climb the leaderboard
